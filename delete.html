Delete Your Qalam Account

App: Qalam — قلم: Arabic Handwriting (com.technion.qalam)

You can request the deletion of your Qalam parent account and all data associated with it at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to qalam1481@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my Qalam account".
  2. Send it from the email address your parent account is registered with (or include that address in the message), so we can verify the request.
  3. We will confirm and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • The parent account and its email address.
  • The child profile (nickname) and all practice progress (letters practiced, repetitions, mastery records).
  • All cloud data associated with the account.

What is retained

  • Nothing tied to your identity. Technical service logs, which contain no names, no emails, and no handwriting, expire automatically within 30 days under our infrastructure's standard retention.
  • Data stored only on your device (local practice data) is not on our servers — remove it by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage.
