<objective>
Build the first three Letter-Unit sections on top of the engine, pixel-faithful to the prototype:
**Meet the letter** (Section 1 — the teachCard with the sound, the door image, and the four-forms
morph strip ported from Shape-Shifter; PromptHeader-only, nothing to write), **Watch & Trace**
(Section 2 — the stroke-order demo then trace the isolated baa over the dotted guide), and **Forms in
context** (Section 3 — trace initial/medial/final, then join them into باب). Each section is assembled
by feeding the relevant baa Exercise configs into the 5 components from Plan 07-04 — no bespoke
screens, no new grading UI. Play buttons play bundled clips offline via the Plan 07-02 audio player.

Purpose: deliver the front half of the baa unit as real, scored, audio-backed vertical slices — the
"watch a letter, trace it, learn its forms" experience. Output: 3 section widgets + ARB copy + widget
tests, all driven by config through the engine (CUR-01) with offline audio (S1-06).
