<objective>
Bundle the existing Qalam brand assets — the reed-pen **mascot** (5 poses) and the brand
**icons** (star, lock, qalam-nib, check-complete, ink-drop) — from the design kit into the
app's `assets/` tree, and register `assets/mascot/` in `pubspec.yaml`. These SVGs already
exist under `docs/design/kit/project/assets/` but were never copied into the shipping app
(only a text wordmark `assets/logo.svg` is bundled today; `assets/icons/` holds only a
`.gitkeep`). Without this, the demo home (03-05) and the mastery celebration (03-04) fall
back to a blank/wordmark where the charming mascot and the mastery star should be — the
single biggest hit to demo fidelity for the 2026-06-02 course-staff meeting (D-17).

This is a pure asset-bundling step: copy the SVGs verbatim and register the new directory.
No Dart, no logic, no new packages. It runs in Wave 1 (no dependencies) so every later
wave's screen can reference the real assets.

Output: 5 mascot SVGs under `assets/mascot/`, 5 icon SVGs under `assets/icons/`, and a
`- assets/mascot/` line under `flutter.assets` in `pubspec.yaml`.
</objective>
