by `(childProfileId, letterId)` in one v6→v7 migration, carry `childProfileId` as a filtered

governs this and every future progress table. `ChildProfileMirror`/`child_models/{uid}` stay
uid-keyed (D-17, per-child cloud deferred).

because keys omit `childProfileId`) as a first-class identity rule, not a patch. This is the
phase's highest-risk, atomic plan: the schema change, the migration, the `LetterReps` drop, and
the threading must land together (the readers were already folded in 19-04, which this depends on).
Output: ADR-018, schema v7 + migration, threaded repos/controller, and the 19-01 two-profile
migration test green.
</objective>
