<objective>
Wire the pure `SelectionPolicy` into the live selection path so the two-timescale brain actually
drives what the child sees next — online and offline identically (D-09/D-11). The controller invokes
the policy once per feedback moment, feeds the narrowed candidates to the wire (the coach picks among
them) and to the walker (offline pick), persists the arc to Drift (D-12), and applies the D-08
micro-drill pass rule. The rails hold (R5): the selector accepts an agent pick only if it is a policy
candidate AND graph-legal, else it degrades to the walker.

Purpose: turn the anti-boredom + arc + micro-drill intelligence from a pure library into the running
experience, with zero regression to the offline floor.
Output: candidate-aware selector + walker, controller orchestration + arc persistence, D-08 drill
scoring, offline WHY template.
</objective>
