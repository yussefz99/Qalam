# Phase 8 — Content Prep Plan (run in parallel with Phase 7)

**Goal:** prepare all of Phase 8's curriculum **content** now, while Phase 7 builds the engine —
so that the moment the engine lands, authoring + sign-off is fast, not a cold start.

**Principle:** do the **longest-lead, engine-independent** work first; **Claude drafts, the
owner's mother reviews and signs off** (nothing model-authored ships unsigned).

**Owners:** [C] Claude · [O] Owner (Rami) · [M] Mother (curriculum authority)

---

## What does NOT depend on Phase 7 (can start today)
Audio recordings, illustrations, the Drive-to-data mining, the content drafts, and the mother's
pedagogy decisions are all **independent of the engine**. Only the *tablet form-tracing* and the
*final wiring* need Phase 7. So ~80% of Phase 8 content can be ready before Phase 7 finishes.
